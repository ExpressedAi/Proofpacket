LOW Principle Overview

Low-Order Wins (LOW) states that lower-order relations persist under renormalisation. Every Clay program integrates LOW as the primary guardrail.

LOW Core Tenets

  • Δ-Decomposition

    Express systems in primitive Δ relationships before any analytic manipulation.

  • Symmetry Preservation

    Maintain conjugate symmetry across transformations; flag deviations in telemetry.

  • RG Persistence

    Validate that Δ relationships survive coarse-grain ladders with bounded decay.
